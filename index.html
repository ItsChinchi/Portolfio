Who I Am
I make games.
With Unreal
& Unity.

I'm Ahmed — a Computer Science graduate from Alexandria, Egypt, with a focus on game development using both Unreal Engine 5 and Unity. I enjoy the whole craft: designing mechanics, writing systems in C++ and C#, and tuning the small details that make a game feel good to play.

I work with Blueprints for rapid iteration and C++ for systems that need performance and structure. In Unity I build with C#, focusing on tight game loops and responsive controls. My goal is to keep leveling up toward AAA-quality systems work.

Currently working on my next Unreal project — deeper into C++ gameplay systems, combat architecture, and AI behavior.
